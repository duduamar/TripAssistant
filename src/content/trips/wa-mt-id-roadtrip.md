# DAY-BY-DAY PLAN

## Day 1 (Sat, Jul 25, 2026) – Bothell → Leavenworth → Spokane

**Drive:** ~6.5–7 hrs

Highlight photo: [Leavenworth Adventure Park Alpine Coaster](https://www.leavenworthadventurepark.com/tumwatertwister-alpinecoaster)

![Leavenworth Adventure Park Alpine Coaster](https://static.wixstatic.com/media/3199ff_669cc015cc994c318f6a8c53ec8d02dc~mv2.png/v1/fill/w_812,h_561,al_c/3199ff_669cc015cc994c318f6a8c53ec8d02dc~mv2.png)

**Stops:**

- [Leavenworth](https://leavenworth.org/visitors/)
  - Quick stop only
  - [Leavenworth Adventure Park Alpine Coaster](https://www.leavenworthadventurepark.com/tickets-pricing)
  - Short stretch / snack break in town if time allows
- [Coulee Corridor Scenic Byway](https://www.recreation.gov/gateways/13620)
  - Drive from Coulee City north on WA-155 through the Grand Coulee and along [Banks Lake](https://www.recreation.gov/camping/gateways/1206)
- [Steamboat Rock State Park](https://parks.wa.gov/find-parks/state-parks/steamboat-rock-state-park)
  - Short Banks Lake stop for shoreline views / quick stretch
  - If time allows, quick viewpoint at [Crown Point](https://parks.wa.gov/find-parks/state-parks/steamboat-rock-state-park)
- [Grand Coulee Dam Visitor Center](https://www.usbr.gov/pn/grandcoulee/visit/gcvc.html)
  - Hands-on exhibits and dam viewpoints
  - Optional [guided tour](https://www.usbr.gov/pn/grandcoulee/visit/tour.html) if timing works

**Drive legs (approx, includes +20% buffer):**

- Bothell → Leavenworth: ~2h25
- Leavenworth → Coulee City: ~1h40
- Coulee City → Steamboat Rock / Banks Lake: ~25 min
- Steamboat Rock / Banks Lake → Grand Coulee Dam: ~35 min
- Grand Coulee Dam → Spokane: ~1h50

**Stay:** [Tru by Hilton Spokane Valley](https://www.hilton.com/en/hotels/gegsvru-tru-spokane-valley/hotel-info/) (Spokane Valley)

### Notes / Ideas to Refine

- Buy [Leavenworth Adventure Park Alpine Coaster](https://www.leavenworthadventurepark.com/tickets-pricing) tickets in advance.
- [Leavenworth Adventure Park](https://www.leavenworthadventurepark.com/tickets-pricing): Alpine Coaster reservations are required, every rider needs a ticket, and prices are listed before sales tax.
- [Grand Coulee Dam Visitor Center](https://www.usbr.gov/pn/grandcoulee/visit/gcvc.html) is open daily 8:30 a.m.–5 p.m.; admission is free.
- [Grand Coulee Dam](https://www.usbr.gov/pn/grandcoulee/visit/index.html): the laser light show and public tours resume May 22, 2026; tours are first-come, first-served and can be canceled without notice.
- Bring a [Discover Pass](https://www.discoverpass.wa.gov/) if you park at [Steamboat Rock State Park](https://parks.wa.gov/find-parks/state-parks/steamboat-rock-state-park), Crown Point, or Northrup Canyon.
- Optional: Poker at [Northern Quest Poker Room](https://www.northernquest.com/casino/poker) (Spokane night).

## Day 6 (Thu, Jul 30, 2026) – Glacier National Park (from Kalispell)

**Plan:** Full day, short hikes + big views (pick 2–3)

Highlight photo: [Going‑to‑the‑Sun Road](https://www.nps.gov/glac/planyourvisit/goingtothesunroad.htm)

![Going-to-the-Sun Road view](https://www.nps.gov/common/uploads/grid_builder/glac/crop16_9/3D46CA45-B6DC-4792-73610CD68BB6FC28.jpg?width=640&quality=90&mode=crop)

**Morning (west side / Lake McDonald area):**

- [Trail of the Cedars](https://www.nps.gov/glac/planyourvisit/hikinglakemcdonald.htm) (short, accessible nature trail).

**Midday (Going‑to‑the‑Sun Road scenic drive):**

- [Going‑to‑the‑Sun Road](https://www.nps.gov/thingstodo/drive-gtsr.htm) with scenic pullouts.
- [Hidden Lake Overlook](https://www.nps.gov/glac/planyourvisit/hikingstmary.htm) from Logan Pass Visitor Center (shorter alpine hike with views).
  - 1.5 mile hike, we can skip if day is too long

**Afternoon (east side option, if you drive over):**

- [St. Mary Falls](https://www.nps.gov/glac/planyourvisit/hikingstmary.htm) (short waterfall hike).
- [Sun Point Nature Trail](https://www.nps.gov/glac/planyourvisit/hikingstmary.htm) (short walk with lake views).

**Drive legs (approx, includes +20% buffer):**

- Kalispell → West Glacier area: ~55 min
- In-park drives between major stops: ~40–110 min (vary by traffic and parking)
- Return to Kalispell via the **outside loop** (St. Mary → US‑89/US‑2 → Kalispell): ~3h with buffer

**Stay:** [Holiday Inn Express Hotel & Suites Kalispell](https://www.ihg.com/holidayinnexpress/hotels/us/en/kalispell/fcamt/hoteldetail) (Kalispell)

### Notes / Ideas to Refine

- Check **current conditions** and **trail status** before your visit: [Current Conditions](https://www.nps.gov/glac/planyourvisit/conditions.htm) and [Trail Status Reports](https://www.nps.gov/glac/planyourvisit/trailstatusreports.htm).
- 2026 access note (latest official update): **No vehicle reservations required in 2026**, but standard entrance fees still apply. Verify on the official page closer to your trip: [Visiting Glacier in 2026](https://www.nps.gov/glac/planyourvisit/2026-visiting.htm).
